Under the sun on a dusty ground,
The echo of leather on willow resounds.
A bowler runs with fire in his eyes,
While the crowd waits in hopeful surprise.

The batsman stands calm, steady and still,
Guarding his wicket with patience and skill.
One perfect swing, the ball takes flight,
Soaring high in pure delight.

Fielders chase across the green,
In a dance of speed, sharp and keen.
Cheers erupt as boundaries roar,
The crowd demands a little more.

From village streets to grand stadium light,
Cricket unites hearts in thrilling fight.
Not just a game of bat and ball,
But passion, pride, and joy for all. 🌟
